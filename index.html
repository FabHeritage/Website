Desmond Crimson

Desmond's story

Desmond Crimson had a smooth red face and nice gold details, showing high-class style. He enjoyed big parties and dinners with candles, always resting on the wrist of someone who liked his classy look. For him, each tick was like a dance, and every second was a party of luxury.

Dempsey the sporty one

Dempsey's story

Dempsey, nicknamed "The Sporty One," was Desmond’s polar opposite. Designed for adventure, he was rugged, water-resistant, and practically indestructible. Dempsey thrived on the thrill of the race, cheering on his wearer as they conquered marathons, scaled mountains, and swam through roaring waves.

Arthur the oldtimer

Arthur's story

Arthur the Oldtimerwas a relic from another age, his worn leather strap and complex cogs and gears telling tales of countless bygone eras he had lived through, of being worn by scholars and poets and dreamers. His beat was slower, more measured-an admonition to live a moment at a time.
